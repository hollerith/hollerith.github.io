#### Bar chart racing

What with Global Climate disaster and COVID, the bar chart race has become a ubiquitous harbinger of doom for spelling out in this, the age of denial and disinformation, the stark challenges facing us. 

This demo uses vanillajs to fetch and graph grouped/ordered data by rendering an svg. Most graphing libraries like d3.js and chart.js can do likewise. It can be dropped in any html as a unit or widget and the scale can be altered. I've used a raw data set here and excluded results less than 1000. It's easy enough to adjust the width, the viewport, the speed of the run. 

Todo work is add axis, stop, play, forward buttons, and drill downs for each bar.
