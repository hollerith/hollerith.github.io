#### Trendy charts

What with global climate disaster and COVID, the bar chart race has become a ubiquitous harbinger of doom for spelling out, in this the age of denial, the stark challenges facing us.

This demo uses vanillajs to fetch and graph grouped/ordered data by rendering an svg. Most graphing libraries like d3.js and chart.js can do likewise. It can be dropped in any html as a widget as I was aiming for a generic as possible component. I've used a raw data set here and excluded results less than 1000. It's easy enough to adjust the width, the viewport, the speed of the run. The fact the data is ordered makes the logic much simpler. It is not the job of the client to clean and sort data although obviously it could.
