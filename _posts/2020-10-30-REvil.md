#### Are REvil feeling the heat?

REvil gang spokesperson Unkn or Unknown wistfully accepts that travel outside Russia could be
very dangerous for them. 

Indeed what is the point you would think of turning over hundreds of millions of dollars in monero annually 
if you are limited to the toxic gangster circles of Moscow and St Petersberg? 

![hackers]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/unknown.png)

Too great a show of wealth in Russia could itself draw unwanted attention from less successful but powerfully 
placed banditi. When you live in a fascist regime where extra-judicial killings are the norm rather than the 
exception a healthy bank balance does not come with the normal feeling of security it should. 

Stories like those of Eugene Nikulin only reinforce the notion that despite multi-millionaire status it
could all come crashing down in an instant, as they observe darkly, alive but in the hands of the FBI 
or dead at the hands of their competitors or the regime. Indeed the Kremlin's eye is never far from their 
operations and paranoia that some gang members are FSB nannies abide. 

![arrest]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/robocop.png)

#### RaaS - Ransomware as a Service

Despite the huge success of their operation due to the apparent complete inability of Western companies 
and institutions to secure their data from even run-of-the-mill consumer exploits, the overworked gang 
are constantly seeking to evolve by copying new techniques from other gangs and to adapt their business
model, expanding their RaaS - or Ransomware as a Service so they can pivot to selling tooling and training 
to other gangs. This outsourcing of work will no doubt reap dividends where they simply do not have enough
time in the day to exploit all the targets available to them and also to allow them to put some distance 
between themselves and the heat.

![beach]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/beach.png)

Perhaps even they (and their girlfriends) might dream that if their operational security has been good enough 
they might even be able to risk travel abroad in order to spend some of their ill-gotten gains in Paris, Milan,
London or New York. 

No doubt Western intelligence services and law enforcement would love them to try being as they are currently
out of their reach.
